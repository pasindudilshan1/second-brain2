## Graph View Instructions for Obsidian

1. **Open Graph View:** Click the graph icon in Obsidian's left sidebar
2. **Filter by path:** Use `wiki/key-ideas/**` to see only idea nodes
3. **Filter by path:** Use `wiki/article-notes/**` to see only article nodes
4. **Full network:** Use `wiki/**` to see all connections

### Recommended Graph Settings

- **Node size:** By number of connections (larger = more connected)
- **Link distance:** Shorter for clearer clustering
- **Show arrows:** Off (connections are bidirectional)
- **Color groups:**
  - Key ideas: One color (e.g., blue)
  - Article notes: Another color (e.g., gray)

### Key Connections to Visualize

The graph should show:
- **CFO Strategic Leadership** as the most connected central node
- **AI Adoption** as the second hub, connected to most articles
- **Talent Gap** and **Human+Agent** as bridge ideas between AI and leadership
- Articles positioned between the ideas they share
